# Cinematic Terms

Use this file to add professional visual control while still explaining terms in plain language when useful. Pick only terms that help the requested scene.

## Lens And Focal Length

- `14-18mm超广角`: exaggerates scale and depth; useful for giant temples, cities, interiors, impossible spaces. Risk: edge distortion.
- `20-24mm广角`: makes architecture feel huge while staying believable; good for epic buildings, streets, halls, cloud palaces.
- `28mm环境人像`: includes character and environment; good for follow shots and travel-through-space scenes.
- `35mm自然叙事视角`: close to normal human perception; good for realistic tracking shots and story scenes.
- `50mm标准镜头`: neutral, realistic, less exaggerated; good for grounded drama.
- `70-85mm长焦`: compresses background and isolates subject; good for emotional close-ups, portraits, distant palace layers.
- `100mm微距`: close material detail: water drops, fabric texture, product surface, food, jewelry.

Plain-language examples:

- "20mm广角低机位" means the building looks taller and more overwhelming.
- "35mm自然视角" means the camera feels like a real person walking behind the subject.
- "85mm长焦压缩空间" means background layers stack closer together and the subject stands out.

## Shot Size

- `大远景`: establishes scale; good for cloud seas, cities, armies, palace groups.
- `远景`: shows full body and environment.
- `全景`: full body, readable action.
- `中景`: upper body and interaction.
- `近景`: face and emotion.
- `特写`: eyes, hands, product detail, texture.
- `大特写`: intense micro-expression or material detail.

## Camera Movement

- `稳定器跟拍`: smooth walking camera following a subject; good for immersive realism.
- `背后跟拍`: camera follows from behind; good for entering spaces and mystery.
- `低机位仰拍`: makes people/buildings look taller, heavier, more sacred.
- `缓慢推镜`: camera moves forward to reveal importance.
- `拉镜`: camera moves backward to reveal larger environment.
- `横移/滑轨`: camera moves sideways, revealing parallax and depth.
- `环绕`: camera moves around subject; good for product, hero reveal, magic transformation.
- `航拍俯冲`: high-scale movement; good for vast spaces, but can feel CG if overused.
- `手持轻晃`: realistic documentary immediacy; avoid for majestic stable scenes unless requested.

## Depth And Space

- `空气透视`: far objects become lighter, bluer, softer because of haze and distance; makes space feel deep and real.
- `真实景深`: focus behaves like a real lens; foreground/background blur naturally.
- `浅景深`: background blurred, subject emphasized.
- `深景深`: foreground, middle, and background all readable; good for architecture and world-building.
- `遮挡关系`: objects pass in front of each other naturally; key for stable spatial continuity.
- `视差`: foreground moves faster than background as the camera moves; helps realism.

## Light And Atmosphere

- `丁达尔效应`: visible light beams through mist, dust, or clouds; good for temples, forests, morning interiors.
- `体积光`: light has thickness in fog/smoke/clouds; makes scenes cinematic.
- `逆光`: light behind subject, creating rim highlights; good for heroic silhouettes.
- `侧逆光`: half-back light, dimensional and natural.
- `黄金时刻`: warm low-angle sunrise/sunset light.
- `漫反射柔光`: soft, even, realistic light; good for faces and misty scenes.
- `高反差光`: strong highlights and shadows; dramatic but can look stylized.
- `云雾散射`: light softened by cloud/fog; useful for heavenly scenes.

## Color And Texture

- `低饱和高级灰`: restrained, filmic, less plastic.
- `金红暖调`: sacred, palace, sunset, mythic grandeur.
- `青绿冷调`: mystery, jade, mist, ancient landscape.
- `胶片颗粒`: subtle film texture; use "轻微" for realism.
- `真实材质`: stone grain, wet wood, metal patina, fabric weave, jade reflection.
- `自然运动模糊`: moving cloth, hands, crowds blur slightly like real footage.

## Useful Combos

- Giant architecture: `20mm广角 + 低机位仰拍 + 深景深 + 空气透视 + 丁达尔效应`
- Realistic follow shot: `35mm自然视角 + 稳定器背后跟拍 + 真实景深 + 视差 + 自然运动模糊`
- Sacred temple interior: `侧逆光 + 丁达尔效应 + 体积光 + 漫反射柔光 + 空气尘埃`
- Emotional character: `70-85mm长焦 + 浅景深 + 逆光轮廓 + 微表情 + 呼吸起伏`
- Product ad: `100mm微距 + 硬边高光 + 材质反射 + 轻微慢动作 + 干净背景`

## How To Explain To Users

If the user is learning, add a short note after the prompt:

```text
我用了20mm广角和低机位仰拍，让建筑更宏大；用了空气透视和丁达尔效应，让远处宫殿和光束更像实拍。
```

If the user asked for "only prompt", omit explanations.
